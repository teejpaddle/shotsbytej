About SBT

Capturing Every
Defining Moment

Shots By Tej delivers high-quality sports photography and videography tailored for athletes, teams, and organizations. From youth leagues to professional events — every frame tells a story.

01

Sports Photography

High-resolution action shots capturing the intensity, emotion, and peak moments of any sporting event.

  • Full game or event coverage
  • Edited high-res digital gallery
  • Same-week delivery
  • Print-ready files included
  • Online gallery sharing link
02

Sports Videography

Cinematic game film and highlight reels that showcase your team or player at their absolute best.

  • Full HD / 4K game film
  • Highlight reel editing
  • Color grading & sound design
  • Social-ready cuts (Reels/TikTok)
  • Downloadable MP4 delivery
03

Player Profiles

Dedicated photo + video sessions designed to build an athlete's personal brand and recruiting profile.

  • 1-on-1 portrait session
  • Action & lifestyle shots
  • 30-second promo video
  • Social media asset pack
  • Recruiting-ready deliverables
04

Team Coverage

Seasonal or multi-event packages for teams who want consistent, professional coverage all year long.

  • Season-long contracts available
  • Home & away coverage
  • Team photo day included
  • Priority scheduling
  • Discounted bulk pricing
05

Tournament Coverage

Multi-day, multi-team event coverage for tournaments, showcases, and championship events.

  • All-day on-site presence
  • Multiple games per day
  • Per-team gallery delivery
  • Event recap video
  • Organizer licensing rights
06

Social Media Content

Purpose-built photo and video content optimized for Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter/X engagement.

  • Vertical & square crops
  • Branded graphic overlays
  • Stories & Reels edits
  • Caption copy included
  • Posted content rights
